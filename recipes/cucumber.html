Korean Pickled Cucumbers

Description

Spicy Korean cucumber pickles, called oi muchim, are a typical side dish.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Suger
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Gochugaru

Steps

  1. Cut cucumbers into wedges
  2. Sprinkle cucumbers with sugar, salt and gochugaru, toss to combine
  3. Add vinegar to cucumbers and stir
  4. Let cucumbers sit for at least 20 minutes before serving
