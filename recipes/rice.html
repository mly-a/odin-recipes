Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Rice fried with kimchi and egg.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sesame oil
  • Egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in pan. Add in kimchi
  2. Stir in rice, sesame oil, and kimchi juice
  3. Fry an egg and serve on top of rice
  4. Top with green onions
