Kimchi Tofu Stew

Description

Korean stew made with soft tofu and kimchi.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Gochujang
  • Chicken broth or water
  • Enoki mushrooms
  • Soft tofu
  • Egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Add oil to pot. Once hot, add kimchi, gochujang, and chicken broth/water
  2. Once boiling, add soft tofu, mushrooms, egg, and green onions
  3. Serve with rice
